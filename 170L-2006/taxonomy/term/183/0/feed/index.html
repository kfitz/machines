At the beginning of this semester, I was talking with one of my thesis readers, and she recommended that, during this year, I try to be thinking about James with every paper I write. She said this, and I felt myself do the thing -- wide eyes and two or three quick head nods -- that means interest and assent, all the while thinking, "Is she nuts?
