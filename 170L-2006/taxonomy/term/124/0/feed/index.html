I don't know how else to put it. I enjoyed reading this poem. The lines are layered, meaning that the lines of each stanza can change up to six times. Thus the stanza can be read in lots of different ways, yet they all make sense. It's pretty short, so I suggest you check it out.<br /> Like "Rain" it wasn't difficult to understand, but the format was aesthetically pleasing and really clean.
