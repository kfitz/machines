Writing Machines - searching http://machines.pomona.edu.170L-2006/taxonomy/term/25/0 en Not all who wander are lost http://machines.pomona.edu.170L-2006/node/24 <p>I came across this rather common quote three years ago at a gas station off of Highway 101. A group of high school friends and I were driving out to go on a backpacking trip to celebrate the end of our senior year. We stopped at a gas station near the trailhead to fill up the vehicles, and as I got out to stretch I noticed this singular rusted-out VW Beetle parked (well, perhaps I assumed that the situation was less intentional and more permanent than "parked") next to the garage. Blazoned on its bumper was the above saying, and it is one that has stuck with me.</p> <p>The feeling of <em>wandering</em> was one that I had to overcome several times while on my most recent journey through the wide world of blogs. I've spent some time on personal blogs, and I definitely appreciated <a href="http://machines.pomona.edu.170L/blog/3">Gunslinger1818</a>'s comments on <a href="http://www.facebook.com">Facebook</a> and <a href="http://machines.pomona.edu.170L/blog/7">marmalade</a>'s on <a href="http://postsecret.blogspot.com">Postsecret</a>. Unfortunately, this took some of the wind out of my sails and I decided to search for some new blogs.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.pomona.edu.170L-2006/node/24">read more</a></p> http://machines.pomona.edu.170L-2006/node/24#comments searching Wed, 06 Sep 2006 17:13:41 +0000 crashingintowalls 24 at http://machines.pomona.edu.170L-2006
