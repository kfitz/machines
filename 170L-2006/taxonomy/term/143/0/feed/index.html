Writing about these examples, Jenkins says, "Often such works exist on the outer borders of literature." This is correct to be sure, but I would say that spatial storytelling has more of a place in"great literature" than Jenkins gives it credit for. What is a picaresque novel if not an example of spatial storytelling?"
