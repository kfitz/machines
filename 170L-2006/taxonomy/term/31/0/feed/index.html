I noted that Berube's blog was not mentioned in the author's biography at the bottom of the article.</p> <p>In this piece, Berube argues that although there are indeed many more liberal than conservative college professors (the ratio is something like 3 to 1), this is not because liberal academics are trying to keep conservative academics out of their departments, as conservatives like David Horowitz maintain.
