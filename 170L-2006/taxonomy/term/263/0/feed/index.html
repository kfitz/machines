In the article Varian states that old media execs have missed the boat by focusing so much on piracy. What the should really be worried about is the "falling cost of producing and distributing digital content."</p> <p>The salaries of megastars such as Cruise are forms of "economic rent" and their dollar amount is determined by "scarcity." There's only one Tom Cruise and consumer demand for him is high. But, as Varian points out, more and more free time of consumers is spent watching free content on the net, "there are only 24 hours in a day.
