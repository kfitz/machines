Pioneered by Lawrence Lessig, a law professor at Stanford University, the Free Culture movement concentrates on a number of things, the most interesting of which (to me, of course) is the concept of proprietary culture.</p> <p>But wait, you said this was about Free Culture! Well yes, it is, but only because of it's exclusive opposite, the proprietary culture.
