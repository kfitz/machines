While I don't usually read all the articles my mom saves up, I decided to procrastinate and read it through. The author is lamenting the loss of a group of young writers who will define the current generation. She begins the article by saying David Foster Wallace, Jonathan Franzen and others are a little old to be defining the current young generation.
