By moving the screen back &amp; forth, it either gets brighter than or darker than the rest of the screen. Watermarks on media that would scream, shrivel, and die if actually in contact with water amuse me. And it disappears if I get just the right angle...</p> <p>Anyways, I'm used to lj, which, even if you're posting to a community and thus don't get to design the details of the journal yourself (The fact that this is not my personal aesthetic is making it somewhat uncomfortable to post here, it somehow doesn't feel like I can entirely post my thoughts, even though the clean, functional format of the class blog works very well with the sort of writing one is meant to put here), most everyone has a user icon (or several).
