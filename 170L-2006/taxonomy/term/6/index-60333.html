wiki confusion

Submitted by tophat1 on 22 September 2006 - 1:29pm.
  • wiki

I have been monitoring the wiki but I'm not totally sure where we're going with it. It seems like we're heading toward a frame story set-up. Are we each going to tell a random story or should we look for more continuity? I'm asking these as genuine questions since I'm a little confused. I know the wiki should theoretically take on a life of its own and evolve over the course of the semester, but maybe we should have a bit more of a foundation. At any rate, if people could weigh in on the direction of the stories each of us will tell (or if each of us should tell his/her own story), that would be helpful.

Wikipedia Within Wikipedia

Submitted by crashingintowalls on 18 September 2006 - 10:55am.
  • wiki

So, I take it by the sparseness of discussion on our class wiki that I'm not the only one for whom the assignment is daunting (that or it's just been one of those weekends). I've been looking for a place to begin and it is rather overwhelming. What is interesting and relevant that isn't already out there? So, I scratched down a few ideas and then searched them within Wikipedia. Surprisingly, even things that I didn't expect to be in there (Metacognition, Language, Thought) were there with interesting content. It seemed like I was in for a replay of my first overwhelming venture into the blogsphere.
