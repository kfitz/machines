I just had the strangest
Submitted by jughead on 3 December 2007 - 2:22am.

I just had the strangest experience ever... after posting that blog entry, I found another collaborative drawing site, (http://web.okaygo.co.uk/apps/scratchpad/flashcom/index.htm), that I just got totally absorbed in for the past 20 min. You collectively draw whatever you want at the same time as a bunch of other people, and all of a sudden someone started writing Czech words, and so I said hello in Czech, and it turned into a completely bizarre exchange of Czech vocab words. It caught me off guard, but it was a strangely distant yet personal moment for a random web site. i need to sleeeeep
