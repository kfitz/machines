Another Useful Paragraph-Level Tag

Sometimes you might want to use a blockquote, so that an entire paragraph is indented:

For these purposes, you'd use a blockquote tag, which indents the paragraph on the left-hand side.

Be sure to use a closing tag at the end of the blockquote, or everything after the opening tag will be indented.
