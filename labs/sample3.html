HTML Workshop, page 3

Character-Level Formatting

Sometimes you'll want to put text in italics, sometimes you'll want to put it in bold, and sometimes you'll want to underline something.

Of course, you could do all three at once, but I wouldn't recommend it.

You can also affect the formatting of text by choosing to emphasize it, or to make it strong. Some systems (like our blog commenting system) will only allow you to use emphasis or strong (?), for reasons that have to do with designer control over what a page looks like. As you'll be building your own pages, you don't have to worry about that.

Links

Links can send you to another page, or they can send you to another place on the same page.

The second kind of link uses what's known as an anchor. You can combine the two forms of link by linking to an anchor on another page.
