However, there are ways that Rico acts which suggest that the military is not as glamorous as it seems.</p> <p>Rico mentions on multiple occasions how proud he is to be a part of the Mobile Infantry. "We're all volunteers;" he says, "we're M.I. because we want to be, we're proud to be M.I. and the M.I. is proud of us.
