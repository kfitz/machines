(current judgment=awesome.)<br /> One totally random thought that has occurred to me: why are the powerful, liberated and sexually liberated women in movies always kissing each other? Tank Girl makes out with Jet Girl to help her out of a sticky situation, though she has a boyfriend/partner at the start of the film, so she's not off-limits to men. My question: is this an indication of their freedom from the proper social constructions of gender and heterosexuality, or is it just another male fantasy to augment the eroticization of powerful women? If one adheres to Laura Mulvey's theory that all film is conplicit with the male gaze, the answer would be the latter, but I'm not sure I do agree with her.
