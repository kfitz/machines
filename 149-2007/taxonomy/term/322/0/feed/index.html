postmodernism - negative narcissism http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/taxonomy/term/322/0 en Butler http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/node/169 <p>The discussion of "negative narcissism" is another one of the several examples Butler provides that possesses a very contradictory nature. Negative narcissism deals with the counteractive effect of "regarding oneself as excrement." It is this negative experience that creates a disparity from actually identifying with the excrement. Therefore, a mediator, like a priest, is needed where "everything that the abject consciousness offers, that is, all of its externalizations, including desire, work, and excrement, are to be construed as offerings, as paying penance" (51).</p> <p><a href="http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/node/169">read more</a></p> http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/node/169#comments Butler negative narcissism Sun, 18 Nov 2007 08:21:14 +0000 CA92075 169 at http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007
