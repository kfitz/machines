He highlights the notions of Lacan on his emphasis of the relationship of schizophrenia as one of postmodernism's privative features. Lacan's view is that meaning is not based on a "one-to-one relationship between signifier and signified," but rather, "is generated by the movement from signifier to signifier" (26).
