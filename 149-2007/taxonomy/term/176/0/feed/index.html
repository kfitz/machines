postmodernism - atomic bombs and such http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/taxonomy/term/176/0 en Baudrillard: Balance and Neutrality http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/node/79 <p>In "The Orbital and the Nuclear", Baudrillard introduces the concept of balance and neutralization. However, "balance and neutrality" are not balanced and neutral; they seem to be more of a conscious effort to negate the eminent threat of something more. In the context of the atomic bombs potential threat as a deterrent to its use, Baudrillard claims "deterrence itself is the neutral, implosive violence of metastable systems or systems in involution."
