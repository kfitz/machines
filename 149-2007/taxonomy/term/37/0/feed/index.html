postmodernism - why learn? http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/taxonomy/term/37/0 en the death of the narrative http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/node/14 <p>"Knowledge is and will be produced in order to be sold, it is and will be consumed in order to be valorized in a new production: in both cases, the goal is exchange. Knowledge ceases to be an end in itself, it loses its 'use-value'." (Lyotard 4-5) I believe the second half of this statement to be false and the first half to be inconsequential. As long as liberal arts colleges exist, knowledge will always be an end in itself. This class offers me no material gain, any knowledge I gain will broaden my understanding of the world, but will have little effect on my career in Real Estate.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/node/14">read more</a></p> http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/149-2007/node/14#comments knowledge Lyotard Narrative why learn?
