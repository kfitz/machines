Texts

The following required texts are available at Huntley:

Mary Shelley, Frankenstein, or the Modern Prometheus
Thomas Pynchon, V.
Margaret Atwood, The Handmaid's Tale
Orson Scott Card, Ender's Game
Diana Hacker, A Writer's Reference, 4th edition

Additional required readings are contained in the course packet, available in the English department office. The readings in this packet are on reserve in Honnold, and a copy of the packet is on reserve in the English department library.
