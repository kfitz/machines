Midterm Proposal: Political News

By imanpod - Posted on 21 February 2008 - 9:51pm.
Tagged:
  • midterm
  • Proposal

For my midterm project I want to analyze the site, www.drudgereport.com, a political web site that uses hyperlinks to connect a visitor to large amounts of information from a single page format. Drudge seeks to deliver a general sense of current news without much commentary and using a series of intriguingly titled links to connect one to a more substantive site. The site does not rely solely on its own reporting nor insight, instead using the page as a jumping off point to a multitude of dedicated reporting sites.
