Writing on the Computer

By Cass89 - Posted on 17 February 2008 - 11:15pm.
Tagged:
  • reading response 4

The most interesting part of Theodore Nelson's article was when he talked about the three false theories of writing. Though inspiration is a key tool in writing, hard work is also necessary. It's impossible for a writer to have inspiration and not the drive to dig deeper into their ideas. When writers are inspired, it takes a lot of work for that inspiration to be perceived correctly. Once an idea hits, the perspiration comes from elongating that idea and extracting information. Once a good outline is created, it again takes a lot of work to fill in the missing pieces.
