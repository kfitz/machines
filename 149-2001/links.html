Links

This collection of links is very much in progress. Some of these links are to resources you will need in your daily work; others are just interesting things to check out. Let me know if you find more things that should be added.

Vox Libris - The library. Know it. Use it. Grow to love it.

PopCultures.com - Sarah Zupko's Cultural Studies site. Excellent background materials, including essays on numerous cultural theorists.

Marxists.org - The site of an international association of Marxist scholars. Many online primary texts by dozens of Marxist theorists. Particularly check out the students' introduction to Marxism, and the encyclopedia of Marxist terms.

Theory.org - A slick and informative media theory site. Get your Judith Butler trading cards here.
