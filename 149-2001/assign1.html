Assignment 1
Due Thursday, February 22

Length: 8 pages (with 1" margins, in 12-point Times)

Your first major writing assignment requires you to trace the development of some particular concept out of Marxist theory through at least four of the writers we have read thus far this semester. This may be any concept of your choosing -- such as ideology, reification, commodification, base v. superstructure, etc.

You must in your paper set up a working definition of the concept and discuss its importance to Marxist cultural analysis. You must further explore the similarities and differences between the uses of this concept in the authors you cite -- how does the concept change in importance or in focus over time? How does its meaning shift?

I will be asking you for some intermediate work toward this paper over the next couple of weeks; please come talk to me as you begin focusing your ideas.

Final details: Your paper must be titled, must be in a hard-copy format, must be typed, double-spaced. And it must use MLA format for citations. If you don't know MLA format, look it up.
