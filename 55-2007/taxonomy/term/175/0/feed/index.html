IT'S A BABY! http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/55-2007/node/212 <p>When I read about Mario's birth, I was a little disturbed. "He had to be more or less scraped out, Mario, like the meat of an oyster from a womb to whose sides he'd been found spiderishly clinging...(313)" The description of scraping him out of a womb sounds like an abortion, and it makes him seem like some unwanted parasite. It's strange that he survived such a traumatic birth, and in some ways, maybe it can be interpreted that part of him did die? His eyes have an odd dead gray-green color to them, and his scalp is the same color. I envisioned a semi-decaying body...(is that morbid?) Anyway a question I had, is Mario James' son or Charles'? His hair is likened to that of the "athletic director and Academy Headmaster," (314) and it flaps up in the same way Charles' hair does.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/55-2007/node/212">read more</a></p> http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/55-2007/node/212#comments brotherly love mario Wed, 21 Mar 2007 09:08:41 +0000 sarawrs 212 at http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/55-2007
