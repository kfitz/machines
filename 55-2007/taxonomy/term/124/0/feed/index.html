(323)</p> <p>We've talked about it before, but more and more as we keep reading I think DeLillo seems to be saying that paranoia is a good thing, that establishing connections to things is absolutely necessary. This quote in particular says that making connections that maybe aren't there is actually necessary to have a sense of purpose in life. The connection between the 'enormous' powers of the world and the relative insignificance of your own life is probably not a real one, but still DeLillo says we need it.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/55-2007/node/147">read more</a></p> http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/55-2007/node/147#comments connections paranoia Underworld Wed, 21 Feb 2007 21:10:18 +0000 roses 147 at http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net/55-2007
