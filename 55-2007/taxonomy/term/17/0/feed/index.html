The common view would be that WWII was one of the most coherent wars, evil versus good, plain and simple. (I don't really believe that, as a disclaimer). WWI, on the other hand, was murky and ridiculous and insane--rather like the Vietnam War. By focusing on the end of WWII, Pynchon manages to make it seem as stupid as Vietnam. In fact, it seems like most of the characters don't really talk much about Hitler, or the Nazis--it's not the issue. The rocket is the issue.
