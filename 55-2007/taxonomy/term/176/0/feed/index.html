It made me think of a theory I once heard about the evolution of man. The theory holds that we are no longer homo sapiens but have moved on to a new order of homo: homo ludens. While homo sapiens translates from the latin as "wise man" or "knowing man", homo ludens means "man the player" or "playing man."
