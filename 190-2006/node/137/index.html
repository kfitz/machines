Dr. McDreamy or How I learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb

Submitted by DaLynziiChic on 25 September 2006 - 11:49pm.
  • alien cyborgs
  • Jackass
  • McSteamy
  • McVet
  • Wknd Box Office Report

In case anyone was wondering, JACKASS topped the boxoffice this weekend at $29 M. The next closest movie after that was Jet Li's FEARLESS...at $10.6 M. I can't believe it. It's incredible. A movie where guys drink horse semen and attach leeches to their eyeballs rips the box office a new one. Not to mention the production cost for such a film can't be more than $10M TOPS, and most of that money probably went toward paying the Idiots--I mean "Stars".

Box Office this weekend looked like this:
1) Jackass - 29
2) Fearless - 10.6
3) Gridiron Gang - 9.5
4) Flyboys - 6.0

Hm. Either
1) Only the 15-30 males are seeing movies
-or-
2) Movies are now targeting 15-30 males more than ever.
-or-
3) Maybe they will make a Grey's Anatomy movie!!!!!

Anyhow, I just thought that was interesting. Now I'm sure many ladies enjoy those films as well--I know I do--but I just think it's important to call attention to box office trends...BEFORE IT'S TOO LATE. Maybe a good amount of those numbers were contributed by women. Maybe we are all growing more uniform in our movie tastes, or maybe there just wasn't anything better to see. I just thought I'd bring this up and see if it sparks any discussion.
