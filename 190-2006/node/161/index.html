Kate Moss is Black?

Submitted by racinian on 27 September 2006 - 6:43pm.
  • random thoughts

The Independent, a newspaper out of London, recently put out a special "African Issue" as part of the Red Campaign (www.joinred.com for some guilt-free consumerism!) and sent half of the revenues to fight AIDS in Africa. And on the cover is the notorious Kate Moss, in black paint! I'd love to elaborate on this, but the LSAT prep-book has my name written all over it so I don't have enough time right now. Anyways, here's a link to the article. A lot of readers are upset, calling this a form of racism, and arguing that an African woman should have appeared on the cover, rather than Kate in black-face.

Thoughts?
