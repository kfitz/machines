I love my Net Flix

Submitted by PureJaqassary on 30 September 2006 - 2:58pm.

You know from your title I thought this post was going to be a discussion of porn available through Net Flix and I was going to be shocked...but no.
I have a very particular taste in movies, so I don't know how well my fictional movie recommendations will go over, but I figure documentaries have much broader appeal.

Movies you should watch:
1. Paris is Burning
(documentary: NYC drag queens, houses, drag balls, vouging)

2. Venus Boyz
(documentary: drag kings, gender performance, unbearable cuteness)

3. The Sound and the Fury
(documentary: deaf culture, cochlear implants, family drama)

4. Born Into Brothels
(documentary: brothel system in India, children of the red light district, photography)

5. Devil's Playground
(documentary: Amish rumspringer (time that they go "English" before joining church), Amish gangsters, drug abuse, Meth)

6. Blu, Blanc, et Rouge
(foreign, French: Blue, White, and Red, three seperate films by Polish emigre, moving)

7. Lola Rennt
(foreign, German: Run Lola Run, if you haven't seen it you should, give it all the way through before you decide)

8. Amelie
(foreign, French: another if you haven't seen it you should, adorably sweet but not sacchrine, it's my happy place)

9. Como Agua Para Chocolate
(foreign, Mexican: Like Water for Chocolate, somewhat in the tradition of A Hundred Years of Solitude, but lighter)

10. Hedwig and the Angry Inch
(rock dramedy?: great soundtrack, beautiful, really makes you think, tons of fun)

11. Death to Smoochy
(dark comedy: I don't care what anyone says, this film is brilliant)

If you like those then I have more, if you don't, I think I'm not a good movie resource for you.
