MS 190: Authorship - good news for blind people http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/taxonomy/term/586/0 en Yay for Acrobat http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/510 <p>Maybe I'm behind the times but I had no idea that Adobe Acrobat will read files out loud for you. I was reading "Free Culture" tonight and I wanted to highlight certain passages so I was just looking around the program's toolbar to see if there was any way for me to do that. When I was looking under the "View" tab, I came across an option for "Read Out Loud". So, of course, I had it read the page I was on. It came out in a computerized voice but it still sounded pretty good, I didn't have any trouble understanding it. </p> <p>If this is old news for you, then man, you've been holding out on me.
