With the introduction of internet based social networks, formally known as a social networking services, or SNS, users are allowed to build social networks through a social software and connect to other users based on whatever interests they desire. An internet based social network allows its users to create an identity that may either be acurate or completely inaccurate depending on what they want to portray. Additionally, the overall accessibility to a social networking service is high, therefore allowing almost anyone to join, which may or may not pose a problem in terms of privacy.
