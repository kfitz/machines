"Oh nothing much," I respond, "probably going to do some homework." </p> <p>"Damn" she says, "Because I have four tickets to the Rolling Stones concert that I got at work and no one to go with." </p> <p>Talk about my lucky day. Needless to say I accepted the invitation with little hesitation and several hours later found myself sitting off the third baseline waiting to see the Stones in concert. I don't know how many of you have seen them live, but if you get a chance to go before you die, (or before they die would be more likely), GO! Despite the fact that all four of them are 60 plus years of age, they play and perform with more energy than a group of teenage boys on Adderall.
