We're reading one essay, "Le bebe en brousse" by Nancy Rose Hunt, that details European women's early twentieth century colonial mission in the Belgian Congo. In response to a perceived need for increased black labor in the colony, the European missionaries set out to undermine the native women's tradition of birth spacing.
