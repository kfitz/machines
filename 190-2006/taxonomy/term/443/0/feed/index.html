We buy ringtones, we get the pink RAZR, we put up picture wallpaper, we get those stupid jinglejangles and skins...and that's just for our cell phones! Computers are almost worse.</p> <p>Let's take for instance, my computer, a.k.a. POOTER and s.k.a. OKCOMPUTER. The fact that I have such names is the first hint of its narcissistic power; of its ability to reflect an image of me within its content. On my background is a picture of my horse, Frank. Almost everyone has some sort of personalized wallpaper for their computer and almost always it is a picture of themselves. Sometimes it is an image from Digital Blasphemy, which are usually pretty neat.
