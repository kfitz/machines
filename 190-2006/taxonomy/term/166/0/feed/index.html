And then you put in your street address. In Meetro, instead of creating and having your own buddy list, when you sign on you get a list of the people all around you, down to the 1/4 mile. Creepy enough for you?!?! The idea I think is that maybe when you have a question about say where to get your hair cut in Claremont, you can have it answered easily.
