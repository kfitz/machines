MS 190: Authorship - stuff I think about http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/taxonomy/term/150/0 en Save the children('s television) http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/144 <p>Over the summer I worked for a children's television production company in England called Granada Kids. We primarily produced television shows for ITV which is England's second major television network channel following the BBC. The BBC is government funded while ITV is commercial. While I was working at Granada the department was told it was to be shut down. ITV was no longer making enough profit from children's television to sustain development and production of new shows. They are going to continue making game shows because of the low budget nature, but all other children's television programming was going to be cancelled by next spring. As far as public television goes this leaves the BBC with a monopoly on children's programming.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/144">read more</a></p> http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/144#comments stuff I think about Wed, 27 Sep 2006 07:00:56 +0000 Leesoid 144 at http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006
