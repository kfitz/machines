All of my friends who are in the process of applying for consulting jobs have such an advantage in that the school caters to their needs. If applying to a job I was interested in meant following a specific predetermined formulaic process I wouldn't have so much pent up anxiety about the fact that it is senior year and the impending graduation is approaching.<br /> I want to look for work in children's television and perhaps in advertising but it is just a little disheartening to be surrounded by friends who are perusing more predictable paths.
