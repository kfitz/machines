MS 190: Authorship - downloads http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/taxonomy/term/643/0 en File Sharing http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/550 <p>The more I think about it filesharing, the more uncertain I become about its effects on the music industry. Lessig writes that roughly 28% of Americans older than 12 had downloaded music. I doubt a significant number of people over 55 download anything, which bumps the number of actual to potential users up to maybe 1/2. So who are the people who are downloading music, and who isn't? Everyone I know who is extremely into music downloads almost everything, and buys only the best of the best albums, having listened to a much broader selection. If everyone did this, it could have a natural selection-esque effect on the industry and force artists to constantly come up with innovative music to meet the higher standards.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/550">read more</a></p> http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/550#comments downloads free culture lessig Wed, 29 Nov 2006 09:23:00 +0000 gwen 550 at http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006
