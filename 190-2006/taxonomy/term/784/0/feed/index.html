MS 190: Authorship - critiquing the process of filmmaking http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/taxonomy/term/784/0 en And another thing-- http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/658 <p>Orlan also did a really cool (non-plastic-surgery-related) project where essentially inverted the filmmaking process. Instead of first making a movie, Orlan first made movie posters. She put them around Paris and people became confused when they couldn't find a theater where the supposed film was playing. She made a soundtrack from the (non-existent) movie, a DVD for movie, and even held a forum where famous actors, directors, and critics met and discussed the film! When I heard her talk about this project she hadn't yet actually gotten to the part where she makes the film because apparently she needs to find a producer, but maybe by now production is underway. I actually think it would be kind of neat if she just never makes the film and uses the project as a sort of critique of movie posters, DVDs, soundtracks and movie screenings. When are any of these things ever given a second thought?</p> <p><a href="http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/658">read more</a></p> http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/658#comments critiquing the process of filmmaking Orlan random thoughts Wed, 13 Dec 2006 23:16:47 +0000 ofcabbagesandkings 658 at http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006
