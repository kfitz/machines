MS 190: Authorship - response http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/taxonomy/term/182/0 en Mixed Feelings about Man with a Movie Camera http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/169 <p>Let me just say that on Wednesday, I really enjoyed Man with a Movie Camera. I thought it was incredibly visually engaging and groundbreaking (especially considering it was made in 1929). I left class that night impressed and inspired...</p> <p>BUT the following night I watched Berlin: Symphony of a City in another class, and it totally changed my opinion of Man with a Movie Camera. Remember all that stuff at the beginning about it being a huge, unprecidented experiment? Well... Berlin: Symphony of a City was made in 1927, two years before, and it's actually strikingly similar. No "actors," no "sets," just the city... and film AS film... not trying to reference the theater, literature, etc... along with tons of montage. Although I preferred the visuals in Man with a Movie Camera, I lost some respect for the film after seeing that it wasn't based on a unique concept, which I'd originally been led to believe.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/169">read more</a></p> http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/169#comments response Sat, 30 Sep 2006 09:10:25 +0000 lrob 169 at http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006
