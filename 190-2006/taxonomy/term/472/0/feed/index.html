The Information was released as a CD/DVD combo and the DVD features a music video to accompany each song. I read a review prior to the release of the CD which annoys me now because I can't remember where I read it and also can't remember all that Beck said about the album. However, I do remember that he said he had been wanting to do a CD/DVD combo for a long time now because he thinks that it comes closer to how we should experience music, as this totally sensationally stimulating medium.
