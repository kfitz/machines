Being overwhelmed by different things this semester, I decided to do my paper on something that I am interested in, though not necessarily for my thesis, just because I needed to start doing work on it. I'm writing on Wikipedia. Now I have 20 pages ahead of me to write, and i cannot make myself do it. I have no idea what I am doing.
