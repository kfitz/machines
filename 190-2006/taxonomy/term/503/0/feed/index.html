Here, when you visit, you can find not only a list of what songs you've been listening to lately, but also a ridiculous amount of statistical data, providing you with your top albums, tracks and artists on a weekly, monthly, yearly, or all-time basis. For example, during the week of June 12, 2006, my top three artists were The Velvet Teen, Kaki King, and Stereolab.
