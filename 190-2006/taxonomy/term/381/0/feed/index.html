MS 190: Authorship - Death of a President http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/taxonomy/term/381/0 en DOAP http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/343 <p>Something other than video games:</p> <p>I'm sure a lot of you have heard about the film DEATH OF A PRESIDENT, a faux-doc on the assassination of George W. Bush. It's been destroyed by some critics and hailed by others, and won the International Critics award at the Toronto Fest. </p> <p>Its distributor, Newmarket, is having one hell of a time distributing the film in American markets. Theaters are blackballing them like no other. AMC claims "We don't feel it's appropriate to play a film that depicts the assassination of our current president." (Entertainment Weekly, #905, pg 18) </p> <p>Interestingly enough, Newmarket is responsible for the release of everyone's favorite antisemetic take on HOSTEL...none other than Gibson's THE PASSION OF THE CHRIST. Execs seem comfortable with the limited release, knowing that being blacklisted can greatly aid a film's popularity. People will certainly hear about the film, and if they want to see it, they'll see it.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/343">read more</a></p> http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006/node/343#comments Death of a President sugartits Tue, 31 Oct 2006 08:53:49 +0000 DaLynziiChic 343 at http://machines.pomona.edu.190-2006
