And I realized that both were completely different in terms of who joined and how they portrayed themselves. And then Facebook opened up to the public in an attempt to become more inclusive. And Myspace has recenetly allowed for profiles to be blocked to the public. As has facebook. So myspace is becoming more exclusive and facebook more inclusive. Is this an attempt by both to attract both people who lobe being in the public eye and people who desire a greater level of privacy? How does the interface affect the overall usage?
