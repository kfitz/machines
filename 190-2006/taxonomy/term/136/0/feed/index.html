Of the four main female characters, not one is someone I would want to emulate. We have Scarlett Johansson, who plays the unfaithful, money-hungry sweater, dependent on men from start to finish. Then there's Madeleine - perverted, incestuous, manipulative - completely lacking of moral rectitude. Our third leading lady, and perhaps scariest of them all, Madeleine's mother, is a stunning pillar of deranged hysteria.
