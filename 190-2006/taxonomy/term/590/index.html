Well, I saw BABEL on Wednesday.

And...it was a little disappointing. I'm not saying that it was BAD in any way, but it just didn't live up to my expectations. It is like CRASH but not even as developed, which is hard for me to say because I didn't really like CRASH to begin with.

Now I suppose I have to justify not liking CRASH all that much--
For me, CRASH pretty much went like this: "Oh look at all our crappy racial problems! ahhh they are so messed up! what a world what a world! race problems are soooo lame." AND SCENE. No solution, no words of advice, too many plot lines to tie up nicely. This isn't to say I didn't LIKE CRASH. No, I enjoyed it, then went home to cry myself to sleep about race problems because I was offered no solution other than crying myself to sleep. THAT'S my beef.
