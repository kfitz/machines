Being a huge gamer himself, Toledano said he had noticed that while people are playing video games, they display these extraordinary emotions and reactions that you don't normally see. In addition, some people exhibit these somewhat abnormal ticks when they become completely immersed in a video game.</p> <p>As he so appropriately says on his website, "I wanted to take portraits of people that would reveal a hidden part of their character.
