Introduction to New Media - environmentalism http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net.51-2007/taxonomy/term/139/0 en Environmental effects http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net.51-2007/node/204 <p>None of us have mentioned the environmental effects of recent technologies. Environmentalists generally think of technology as a factor that increases impact on an ecosystem -- in other words, as a negative effect. Here's one statistic that certainly has to do with Media Studies and would begin to suggest otherwise:</p> <p>Newsprint is 1/6 of all US paper production. The Sunday New York Times alone uses some 75,000 trees per edition.</p> <p>Whoa. This is one more great reason to encourage the rise of online news sites.
