I'll summarize it for those of you who don't want to read the lengthy wikipedia entry. The Uncanny Valley is a theory that a Japanese roboticist came up with to explain emotional responses between humans towards robots. Basically, the theory says that as a robot (or any depiction of a human) becomes more humanlike in appearance and motion then the human response towards it will become more positive, until a point is reached where the robot suddenly becomes repulsive to the human - this is the "valley" in the name, where the levels of empathy dip suddenly.</p> <p><a href="http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net.51-2007/node/151">read more</a></p> http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net.51-2007/node/151#comments class discussion immediacy robots Mon, 19 Mar 2007 04:03:49 +0000 allnighter 151 at http://machines.plannedobsolescence.net.51-2007
